Term Plans

Having some fun with graphic design

As part of my work as a youth group coordinator, I've gotten to design the term plan graphics that let our young people know what's going to happen at youth group for the school term.

I've had a bit of fun with this over the years, have a look at what I've come up with!

2025 Term 2

This was made with ProCreate on my iPad. It was very simple to make, not my most creative for sure!

2025 Term 1

This was inspired by the rapper J-Hope's concert graphics, nobody got the reference except for me!

2024 Term 4

We did a bracelet making activity this term! If I had more time I would have made the bracelets less straight but managing all the objects was a nightmare!

2024 Term 3

I said goodbye to Adobe products and made my first project with Affinity Designer! I still think this one looks great!

2024 Term 2

We're going to outer-space! This one marks my move from Photoshop to Illustrator.

2024 Term 1

I still really like this one! I was limited because I only had a license for Photoshop when it really should have been made with vector-editing software.

2023 Term 4

There wasn't a goal in mind for this one, but it I think it looks quite pretty!

2023 Term 3

Taylor Swift was at the height of cultural relevance at this time!

2022 Term 2

Inspired by the idea of the youth "takeover" here's a space invaders/arcade themed graphic!
