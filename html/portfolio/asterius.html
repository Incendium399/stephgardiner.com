Asterius is a project that was completed over 4 weeks during a Certificate III in Design Fundamentals at AIE.

Asterius is a first-person 3D puzzle adventure game inspired by the greek myth of the minotaur. The player is Asterius, the minotaur, who, with the encouragement of his half-sister, attempts to escape the underground labyrinth in which they have been imprisoned by solving the puzzles constructed by the labyrinth's architect Daedalus.

(Ocean and skybox sourced from the Unity Asset Store, Ariadne Sprites created with Avatar Maker)
