Fox Hunt

"Fox Hunt" was developed as part of a design fundamentals unit at AIE.

Fox Hunt is a 3rd-person isometric stealth simulation game. The player takes the role of a fox hunting for food for their cubs. Collecting berries, bugs and rabbits in their backpack, the player must collect enough food to fill their den while avoiding the hunter hellbent on catching them before the sun rises. After the sun rises, it is much harder for the fox to avoid the gaze of the hunter, increasing the difficulty of player's goal greatly.

(3D art assets were sourced from the Unity Asset Store & 2D icons were sourced from Noun Project)
