I worked on "Plant Husbandry" in a team of 3 for a sound design unit.

"Plant Husbandry" is a person-plant growing dating sim where the player takes orders from customers wanting to find their perfect partner. The player grows and waters person-plants with special fertilizer that is created by mixing specific amounts of ingredients that correspond to personality and physical traits. By mixing these fertilizers the player tries to create a customer's dream partner. Depending on how successful the customerâ€™s first date is with the person-plant the player earns increasing amounts of money.
