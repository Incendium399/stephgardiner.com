My Blog

Hi! My name is Steph Gardiner and I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Adelaide studying computer science. I'm also a graduate of the Academy of Interactive Entertainment where I studied Game Design and Production.

I love doing pretty much everything! Level design, greyboxing, prototyping, producing, UI design, narrative design and more.

I like working in teams to develop fun and enaging games for a wide range of genres and target audiences. I'm always open to learning more, taking feedback and tackling challenges.
